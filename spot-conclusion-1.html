Conclusion

Every argument has one. Your job is to find it — with or without a signal word to guide you.

How to play

You'll see an argument. Read it carefully, then pick which option is the conclusion — the claim being defended.

Some arguments will have indicator words to help you. Others won't. Either way, ask yourself: what is this argument trying to convince me of?
